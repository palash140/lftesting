== Description ==

Share Buttons Widget is simple and easy to use.

With Share Buttons Widget you can share your content at Facebook, Twitter, Google, Digg, Delicious and many more!

After plugin activation you will be automatically redirected to your widgets page so you can configure the Share Buttons Widget in your sidebar.
That simple!

If you need help with the plugin or you want to report a bug please refer to http://seo.uk.net/share-buttons-widget/

== Installation ==

1. Upload the zip to 'plugins' directory or from WP Admin -> Plugins -> and search for Link to us.
1. Activate the plugin
1. You will be automatically redirected to your widgets page so you can configure the Share Buttons Widget in your sidebar.

Share Buttons Widget does not have a settings section on Admin page.
